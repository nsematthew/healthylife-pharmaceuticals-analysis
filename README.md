# HealthyLife Pharmaceuticals Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes the performance of HealthyLife Pharmaceuticals using key business and healthcare metrics. It focuses on understanding sales, profitability, costs, and patient outcomes across different products.

## Tools Used
- Tableau  
- Excel

## Dashboard 2: Patient Outcome Explorer
<img width="1155" height="542" alt="patient-outcome-explorer" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ecb0c677-3a1b-4230-ab1f-bf5f78bc1736" />



This dashboard focuses on patient outcomes and market performance. It highlights variations in patient results and market share across different product categories.

## Key Insights
- High sales do not always translate to high profit  
- Rising production and marketing costs impact profitability  
- Patient outcomes vary across products  
- Market share does not always align with patient outcomes
